# Handmade™ — Use Cases

> Your knitting and crochet life, beautifully organized.

Handmade™ brings together everything you need to knit and crochet in peace: row counting, yarn stash tracking, project management, and thoughtful tools designed by crafters who get it.

---

## Free Tier Use Cases

### Project Library
- **Use Case:** Create unlimited projects and track your completed and planned makes.
- **Actor:** Any crafter (knitter or crocheter)
- **Goal:** Maintain an organised overview of all ongoing, planned, and finished projects in one place.

### Pattern Library
- **Use Case:** Upload and organize unlimited patterns.
- **Actor:** Any crafter
- **Goal:** Store and retrieve patterns easily without relying on scattered files, bookmarks, or paper printouts.

### Tool Stash
- **Use Case:** Track all your needles, hooks, and accessories.
- **Actor:** Any crafter
- **Goal:** Know exactly what tools you own, avoiding duplicate purchases and quickly finding the right needle or hook for a project.

### Yarn Stash
- **Use Case:** Track up to 10 yarns by colour, weight, and yardage.
- **Actor:** Free-tier crafter
- **Goal:** Keep a basic inventory of your yarn collection so you can plan projects around what you already have.

### Basic Row Counter
- **Use Case:** One counter per project with undo, goals, and reset.
- **Actor:** Any crafter working on a project
- **Goal:** Accurately track progress through a pattern without losing count, with the ability to undo mistakes and set row goals.

### Standalone Counter
- **Use Case:** Use without a project attached — perfect for quick makes.
- **Actor:** Any crafter doing a simple or impromptu project
- **Goal:** Count rows or repeats on the fly without the overhead of setting up a full project.

### Social Features
- **Use Case:** Follow other crafters, like projects, and explore profiles.
- **Actor:** Any crafter interested in community
- **Goal:** Get inspiration from other makers, discover new patterns, and connect with the crafting community.

## Premium Tier Use Cases

### Multiple Row Counters
- **Use Case:** Track sleeves, repeats, and complex patterns separately.
- **Actor:** Crafter working on multi-part or complex projects
- **Goal:** Maintain independent counts for different sections of a project (e.g., left sleeve vs. right sleeve) without confusion or manual tracking.

### Row Counter Notes
- **Use Case:** Add reminders to specific rows so you never lose your place.
- **Actor:** Any crafter following a pattern with key rows (increases, colour changes, stitch transitions)
- **Goal:** Attach contextual notes to specific row numbers so you know exactly what to do when you reach them.

### PDF Pattern Viewer
- **Use Case:** View patterns side-by-side while counting.
- **Actor:** Any crafter following a digital pattern
- **Goal:** Eliminate the need to switch between apps — read the pattern and count rows in one view.

### Live Activity & Lock Screen Counter (iOS)
- **Use Case:** See your count on the lock screen and Dynamic Island.
- **Actor:** iOS crafter
- **Goal:** Glance at your current row count without unlocking or opening the app, keeping hands free for crafting.

### Knitting Mode
- **Use Case:** Guided step-by-step pattern navigation.
- **Actor:** Any crafter following a structured pattern
- **Goal:** Be walked through a pattern one step at a time, reducing errors and the cognitive load of interpreting complex instructions.

### Project Notes
- **Use Case:** Track modifications and ideas for every WIP.
- **Actor:** Any crafter with works in progress
- **Goal:** Record adjustments, ideas, and learnings per project so nothing is forgotten — especially useful when returning to a project after a break.

### Manual Pattern Guides
- **Use Case:** Create step-by-step pattern guides.
- **Actor:** Crafter who writes or adapts their own patterns
- **Goal:** Break down a pattern into guided steps that can be followed in Knitting Mode.

### Yarn Usage Tracking
- **Use Case:** Track yarn used per project.
- **Actor:** Any crafter managing yarn consumption
- **Goal:** Know exactly how much yarn a project consumed, helping with future planning and budgeting.

### Time Tracking
- **Use Case:** Track progress and see how long each project takes.
- **Actor:** Any crafter who wants visibility into time spent
- **Goal:** Log crafting time per project to better estimate future projects, understand personal pacing, and appreciate the true effort behind each finished piece.

### Unlimited Active Projects
- **Use Case:** Work on as many projects as you like at once.
- **Actor:** Prolific crafter juggling multiple WIPs
- **Goal:** Remove any cap on concurrent projects, supporting the reality that most crafters have several things on the needles at once.

### Unlimited Yarn Stash
- **Use Case:** No cap on your collection.
- **Actor:** Crafter with a large yarn stash (free tier limited to 10)
- **Goal:** Catalogue your entire yarn inventory regardless of size.
