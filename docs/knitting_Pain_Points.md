# LooseLoop — Pain Points & Problem Statements

> Every Knitter's Nightmare...

These are the core user pain points that LooseLoop addresses — the frustrations crafters face today that create the market opportunity for a unified crafting companion app.

---

## 1. Row Counter Keeps Turning Off
**Pain:** Flow interrupted. Rhythm broken. Your place? Gone.

**Problem:** Crafters rely on phone-based row counters, but screen timeouts and app switching cause the counter to reset or become inaccessible. This breaks concentration and leads to lost progress.

---

## 2. Pattern Lives in PDF, Notes Live Elsewhere
**Pain:** Constant switching. Constant scrolling. Zero calm.

**Problem:** Crafters must juggle between a PDF reader for the pattern and a separate app (or paper) for notes and counting. The constant context-switching disrupts the meditative flow that draws people to knitting and crochet in the first place.

---

## 3. Tracking Counts Across Apps and Scrap Paper
**Pain:** Notes, counters, screenshots... a digital treasure hunt.

**Problem:** Without a single source of truth, project information gets fragmented across multiple apps, sticky notes, and screenshots. Finding the right information at the right time becomes a frustrating scavenger hunt.

---

## 4. One Mistake Throws Off Your Whole Project
**Pain:** A missed row, a wrong count, or a lost note = unraveling frustration.

**Problem:** Knitting and crochet are cumulative crafts — a single counting error can cascade into hours of rework. Without reliable, integrated tracking tools, the risk of costly mistakes is high.
